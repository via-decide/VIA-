Step-by-step guide

How to Play the Full Ecosystem

1
Sign in once — credits work everywhere
Log in with Google on any of the three games. Your VIA Credits wallet is tied to your Google account and synced in real-time across all games via Firebase. You never need to sign in twice.
All Games
2
Start with SkillHex if you're a student or job seeker
Complete interview missions (Oxygen Crisis → Power Allocation → Final Approach). Every 10 tokens you earn converts to 1 VIA Credit automatically. A 3-day streak gives +30 tokens = 3 bonus credits. Build your Skill Rating — higher SR unlocks harder missions that pay more.
SkillHex
3
Start with Mars Terminal if you love games & science
Launch into the ISRO Mars Geological Survey. Move your rover, scan rock formations, answer planetary science MCQs. Each correct answer = +3 VIA Credits. Complete a full mission run and your Survival Index (0-100%) multiplies your credit reward. Completing 3 missions unlocks Tier 2.
Mars Terminal
4
Grow your Orchard with credits from the other games
Open Orchard Engine. Each plot costs 50🪙 to seed. Use credits earned in SkillHex or Mars to fund your seeds — you don't have to start from zero. Research your plants to evolve them through 5 stages. Harvest a mature plant for 500-2000🪙 depending on rarity.
Orchard
5
Transfer credits to friends (the social loop)
In Orchard's Market tab, paste any user's UID to send credits. In SkillHex, the leaderboard shows top players — you can gift credits as a challenge. "Send me 50🪙 if you beat my SR score." This turns the games into a social layer on top of VIA's feed.
All Games
6
Breed rare hybrids and sell (Orchard endgame)
Once you have two Mature (Stage 4) plants, use the Bio-Genetic Lab to cross-pollinate them for 100🪙. Hybrids inherit combined traits and have a 20% chance to upgrade rarity. A Legendary plant harvests for 2000+ credits — enough to fund 40 new plot seeds.
Orchard
7
Unlock Mars Tier 2 missions (requires credits)
Complete 3 Tier 1 missions (Dust Season, Cascade Failure basics) to advance. Higher tier missions have tougher constraints but pay significantly more credits per run. Advanced missions require 70%+ Survival Index — credits from SkillHex can subsidize retries.
Mars Terminal
8
Watch your game activity appear in the VIA Feed
Harvesting a Legendary plant, completing a Mars mission, or hitting a SkillHex milestone posts a card to your VIA social feed. Friends can see it, send reactions, or click "Play Now" to jump into the same game. This is the discovery loop — games grow the VIA network.
